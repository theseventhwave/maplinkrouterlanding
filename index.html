Supported links

Know what routes (and what should not).

Supported

  • Safari on iOS and iPadOS
  • Apple Maps, Google Maps, Waze
  • Common link shapes: search, coordinates, directions
  • Best-effort app opening when installed

Not supported / may not redirect

  • Other browsers (Chrome iOS, Firefox iOS, Edge iOS)
  • In-app browsers (Instagram, Gmail, etc.)
  • Short links that need expansion (maps.app.goo.gl)
  • Non-link SPA buttons without an actual href
Private Browsing: Safari may disable extensions unless you enable “Allow in Private Browsing.”

FAQ

Short answers, what to expect.

Does it work outside Safari?

No. MapLink Router only works in Safari with the extension enabled.

Why did a link stay in Safari?

The link may be unsupported, the website is not allowed in Safari settings, or redirects are disabled in extension options.

What about Private Browsing?

Enable “Allow in Private Browsing” for the extension, then reload the page.

The extension settings page won't open.

Force-quit Safari and reopen it, then try again. If it still doesn't appear, toggle the extension off/on in Settings -> Apps -> Safari -> Extensions.

Do you track my browsing?

No. There is no analytics, telemetry, or tracking.

Why does a Google cid= link open Google briefly?

Some Google links require a bounded canonicalization step (up to 3.0 seconds hard max) to resolve the destination safely.
